  • About me - This page is a brief summary of who I am, my interest, what has inspired me to get into web development, and my future goals.
  • Portfolio - This shows a collection of the projects, or certain aspects of said projects, that I am most proud of. This is to demonstrate what I’ve learned and how I use these skills.
  • Resume - Recreation of my resume in HTML that shows my education, experience, and skills.
  • Contact - A way to contact me, whether for questions, or feedback on my work. This will include most of my professional contact information as well as a link to my linkedin profile.
